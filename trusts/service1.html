Coffee Grinding

Your grinder is where a great cup of coffee starts! Take it to the next level!

In Coffee Grinding seminar you build confidence in using the grinder and understand the grinding process, its effect on the cup and the flavor characteristics.

  • We will show you how to set up your grinder
  • How to calibrate the knives
  • Explore all the grinder concepts and get a better understanding on the various grind settings
  • Evaluate the grinding size depending on the extraction, adjust it if needed and retry
  • Learn all about the cleaning & maintenance process

No previous experience is required to join coffee grinding seminar.

Note: You will need to carry a Covid-19 Certificate (Vaccine) or a Covid-19 Certificate (Disease). Otherwise, you need to demonstrate a negative Rapid or PCR Test result up to 48 hours before.
